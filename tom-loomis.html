Why most small Shopify stores plateau

  • Stock theme ceiling — every layout decision was made for someone else's brand. You inherit their compromises.
  • App stack creep — five page builder apps, three review apps, two upsell apps. Each one slows the site and rents you back features Shopify already ships.
  • SEO afterthought — meta tags filled in by hand, no schema, no internal linking strategy, no Search Console.
  • Zero AEO setup — ChatGPT, Gemini, and Google AI Overviews answer customer questions about products like yours, citing competitors instead of you.
  • Mobile performance debt — themes carry years of bloat. LCP and INP fall short of Core Web Vitals on the devices most of your traffic is on.

What a custom build changes

  • The store looks like your brand — typography, layout, voice. Not a template anyone else can buy.
  • Built on Liquid — the right runtime for stores under ~$5M/yr. Hydrogen costs 3–5× more and only pays off at scale.
  • Native features over apps — Shop Pay, subscriptions, gift cards, automatic discounts, abandoned cart — all wired into the theme, no app subscriptions piling up.
  • Performance-tuned from day one — Core Web Vitals targeted before launch, not patched after.
  • Found by humans and by AI — full technical SEO baseline plus AEO content structure so the store gets cited in answer engines while competitors are still figuring out what AEO means.
