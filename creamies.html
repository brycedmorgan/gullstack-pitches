The Strategy
Turn brand love into a DTC revenue machine.

We'll transform creamies.com from a basic Shopify template into a storytelling, SEO-optimized, subscription-powered DTC platform that matches your legendary brand.

  • Fix the 404 About page — Tell the Creamies origin story. This is your most valuable marketing asset

  • Third-party citation building — Get Creamies mentioned on food blogs, review sites, Reddit
  • FAQ schema on every page — Answer the exact questions AI models ask about ice cream
